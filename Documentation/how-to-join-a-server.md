# How to Join a Server

Access to Project Overwatch (POW) is fully automatic! You don't need an invite code or a link to your server's dashboard. POW uses your Discord rank to determine which servers you belong to.

### 1. Link Your Accounts
1. Log into **[pow.ciankelly.xyz](https://pow.ciankelly.xyz)** using **Discord**.
2. Click on your profile icon in the top right and go to **Settings**.
3. Ensure your **Roblox Account** is also linked. This is required for logging shifts and punishments.

### 2. Check Your Discord Rank
1. Ensure you are in your community's Discord server.
2. Verify that you have the **Staff Role** (e.g., Moderator, Trainee, Admin).
3. If you just received your role, Discord can sometimes take a few minutes to update its data.

### 3. Automatic Discovery
1. Go back to the **Dashboard Home Page**.
2. POW will automatically scan all the Discord servers you are in.
3. If it finds a community that uses POW and you have the required staff role, that server will appear on your dashboard instantly!

### 4. Frequency & Refreshing
- **Caching:** POW checks for new servers every **10 minutes**. 
- **Force Refresh:** If you just got a new rank and want to see it immediately, log out and log back into POW. This will force a fresh sync with Discord.

{% hint style="danger" %}
**Still Don't See Your Server?**
If your server doesn't appear after 10 minutes, contact your Server Admin. They may need to update the **Staff Role ID** in the Admin Panel settings or ensure the **POW Bot** is online in their Discord.
{% endhint %}

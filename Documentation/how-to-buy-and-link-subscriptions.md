# How to Buy and Link Subscriptions

Unlock advanced features like Raid Detection, AI Risk Assessment, Data Exports, and White Label Bots by upgrading your server plan.

### 1. Choosing a Plan
1. Visit the **Pricing Page** at **[pow.ciankelly.xyz/pricing](https://pow.ciankelly.xyz/pricing)**.
2. Select the plan that fits your community:
    - **Free:** Basic moderation and logging.
    - **POW Pro:** Adds Raid Detection, AI Insights, and Data Exports.
    - **POW Max:** Adds White Label Bots and prioritized processing.

### 2. Purchasing
1. Click **Upgrade Now** on your chosen plan.
2. You will be redirected to our secure payment processor (Stripe).
3. Complete the checkout process. Once finished, you will be sent back to the POW Dashboard.

### 3. Linking to a Server
Subscriptions are tied to your **User Account**, not a specific server automatically. You must link your purchase to the community you want to upgrade.

1. Go to the **Dashboard Home Page** (the server selector).
2. You will see a prominent banner at the top saying: **"You have an unlinked subscription!"**
3. Click the **Link to Server** button on that banner.
4. Select the server you want to upgrade from the dropdown list.
5. Click **Confirm Link**.

### 4. Managing Your Subscription
You can manage your billing, change plans, or update your payment method at any time.
- **Option A:** Click on your **Profile > Settings > Billing**.
- **Option B:** Go to your server's **Admin Panel > Subscription**.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Switching Servers:** If you close your community and want to move your Pro/Max subscription to a new server, you must first **Unlink** it from the current server's Admin Panel, then repeat the "Linking" steps above.
{% endhint %}

# Player Panel

The **Player Panel** is a centralized view for everything related to a specific player on your server. It is the primary place where staff issue punishments and investigate player history.

## 1. Finding a Player

There are several ways to open a player's panel:

*   **Search Bar:** On the Mod Panel, use the search bar at the top of the player list. Typing at least 3 characters will search online players, recent logs, and the entire Roblox database.
*   **Active Player List:** Click on any player's name in the "Online Players" list.
*   **Live Logs:** Click on any username appearing in the Join, Kill, or Command logs.
*   **Punishment History:** Click on a username in the recent punishments list.

## 2. Viewing Player Information

Once a panel is open, you can see:
*   **Roblox Profile:** Their Display Name, Username, Roblox ID, and Account Age.
*   **Online Status:** Whether they are currently in your PRC server.
*   **In-Game Details:** If online, you can see their current Team, Vehicle, and Discord/Staff status (if applicable).
*   **Punishment History:** A complete, scrollable list of every warning, kick, and ban they have received on your server.
*   **Recent Activity:** A filtered log of their specific joins, kills, and commands.
*   **AI Risk Assessment:** (POW Pro/Max) A summary generated by AI that analyzes their log history to identify suspicious patterns.

## 3. Issuing Punishments

From the Player Panel, you can issue official punishments that are saved to the POW database and synced with Discord.

1.  Click the **Log Punishment** button (or use the Quick Action buttons like Warn, Kick, Ban).
2.  Select the **Type**:
    *   **Warn:** Issues a formal warning.
    *   **Kick:** Removes the player from the server.
    *   **Ban:** Bans the player from the server.
    *   **Ban Bolo:** Marks the player as "Be On Look Out". This is used for players who need to be banned but aren't currently online, or for tracking suspicious individuals.
3.  Enter a **Reason**. Be as descriptive as possible, as this is logged for admin review.
4.  Submit the punishment.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Automation:** Issuing a punishment through the panel can trigger **Automations** (like sending a webhook to a Discord channel) and will be recorded under your staff profile for quota/activity tracking.
{% endhint %}

## Troubleshooting

**"User not found on Roblox and no logs found"**
*   **Fix:** Ensure you have spelled the username exactly correctly. If the user has never joined your server and doesn't exist on Roblox, POW cannot create a panel for them.

**I issued a punishment, but it's not showing up.**
*   **Fix:** Refresh the page. If it still doesn't appear, ensure you have the `canIssueWarnings`, `canKick`, or `canBan` permissions assigned to your Panel Role.

**The user is online, but the panel says OFFLINE.**
*   **Fix:** The online status is updated every 10-15 seconds. Wait a moment for the status to sync with the game server.
